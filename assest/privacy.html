PRIVACY BY DESIGN

  • Implementing approriate technical and organisational measures to protect user's data.
  • Integrate data privacy and security safeguards to protect user's rights.

When to implement

During the design phase of processing activity

PRIVACY BY DEFAULT

  • Enabling privacy-friendly settings when processing personal data
  • Ensure dat minimization and processing achieve specified and lawful purposes only

When to implement

During the lifecycle of any processing activity

Both Privacy by Design and Privacy by Default are approaches that call for integrating privacy and data protection into a business's processing activities and practices, right from the design stage through its entire lifecycle.
